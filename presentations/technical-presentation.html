Efficient MEPF Design

Every building is unique and requires appropriate design features, but some of the measures are highly effective in almost all the building projects like:

  • Sustainability, Reliability & Scalability
  • Must meet all building codes specified by NBC / IS / NFPA / NABH & local government
  • Well-designed MEP system working at optimal performance to promote energy savings
  • Equipment with high efficiency Glare-free lighting
  • Maintaining Good Indoor Air Quality (IAQ)
  • Regulating internal temperatures & humidity
  • Ease of operation & maintenance
  • Flexibility and modularity built into the design for future needs if any
  • Use of latest technology
  • Cost optimization towards both capital and running cost
  • Control of CO2 emissions
  • Post Occupancy Review
