About us

— 20 Years of Sustainable MEPF Design

We (E Sollutions Consultancy Pvt Ltd), with over two decades of experience, delivers world-class MEPF and iBMS consultancy services across India. Since 2004, we’ve partnered with leading clients to build technically sound, energy-efficient spaces.

Our 150+ engineers operate from offices in Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Delhi. We provide integrated Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Protection, and Smart Building design — backed by our in-house BIM team.

E Sollutions designs MEPF systems for IT/ITES campuses, hospitals, data centers, manufacturing, hotels, and high-rise residences. From concept to commissioning, we deliver detail-driven solutions with full lifecycle performance.

Our promise: “To exceed client expectations with customized engineering, quality service, and long-term reliability.”
